Tentang STEM X Football Academy

Kami membekali siswa menjadi pemikir kritis dan pemecah masalah yang percaya diri, dengan pemahaman bahwa sains dan olahraga adalah dua sisi yang saling melengkapi dalam mewujudkan potensi dan prestasi terbaik.

Our Story

Founded by educators and coaches who believed that learning should be as exciting as the game itself.

2019

The Vision

Dr. Sarah Chen, a physics professor and former collegiate soccer player, noticed her students struggling with abstract concepts. Meanwhile, Coach Marcus Rodriguez observed his players making tactical decisions based on instinct rather than analytical thinking. They decided to combine forces.

2020

First Steps

Starting with just 15 students in a local community center, they launched the first pilot program combining physics lessons with football training. The results were immediate and remarkable - students showed 40% improvement in physics grades while developing enhanced game intelligence.

2022

Growth & Recognition

Word spread quickly about the innovative approach. By 2022, the program had grown to 150 students across three locations. The academy received the "Innovative Education Award" and was featured in several educational publications for its groundbreaking methodology.

2025

Future Forward

Today, STEM+Football Academy serves over 300 students with a comprehensive curriculum spanning all STEM subjects and age groups. Our alumni have gone on to excel in both academic and athletic pursuits, with many receiving scholarships to top universities.
