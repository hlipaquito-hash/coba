Types of Football Data

Two Main Categories

Football data generally falls into two main categories: Event Data and Tracking Data. Each type provides different insights and requires different collection methods and analysis techniques.

üìä Event Data

Discrete actions that occur during a match - recorded when they happen.

Examples: Goals, passes, shots, tackles, fouls, corners, free kicks

üéØ Tracking Data

Continuous position data of all players and the ball throughout the match.

Examples: Player positions, speeds, distances, heat maps, movement patterns
